2008-08-02

It seems the blogging world loves to play a good game of tag.  I’ve received two tags in the past few weeks.  So, for those of you looking for some Sunday reading here is way more than you could possibly want to know about little ol' me!!

 

Busy Mamas tagged me over a week ago with this Meme…  (Sorry Busy Mamas for taking so long to respond)!

Think back on the last 15 years of your life. What would you tell someone that you hadn’t seen or talked to for 15 years? How would you sum up your life? You get 10 bullet points. A list of 10 things to summarize you. At the end of your list, tag 5 more people and send on the love.

1.  15 years ago I was 14.  I guess that makes me preparing for my freshman year of high school.  Since it was summer time, I was most likely busy preparing for my first year in the marching band.

2.  In December of 1996, my senior year of High School, I started dating my hubby.  I remember coming home from a date with him on Dec 22 and telling my parents that he "liked me" so we were now "going out" as in he was my boyfriend!!  I was so excited!!  (and still am)

3.  In May of 1997 I graduated High School.  In the fall I started at Towson University and Scott started at Grove City College.  That winter my parents moved to western PA (their hometown).  So the summer after my freshmen year of college I went to my new home in western PA and then started at Slippery Rock University (b/c instate tuition moved to PA with my parents).

4.  OK I picked Slippery Rock mainly b/c it was known for its education program (and I was an education major) and it was 7 miles from Grove City College.  Towson had RUF (Reformed University Fellowship) and I had made lots of great Christian friends and loved it there.  Slippery Rock was a P-A-R-T-Y school and while I attended a Christian fellowship group there it wasn’t the same as RUF and I hated it there.  I pretty much spent every weekend at Grove City (yea to my future sister in law who let me sleep with her on her top bunk almost every weekend) so that winter I decided to suck up my pride and transfer AGAIN… in January of 1999 I started at Grove City College.  I had changed my major from elementary education to English/Secondary Education b/c I loved the English classes I took at Towson and Slippery Rock and professors from both places encouraged me to do so.

BUT, one semester of Brit Lit at Grove City was eye opening to me.  After a lot of praying, thinking, talking… I realized I was much happier working with children and so I changed my major back to elementary education that fall. 

5.  In May of 2001 I graduated from Grove City College with a degree in Elementary Education and 5 schools on my college transcripts (Towson, Slippery Rock, Community College of Beaver County and the Community College of Allegheny County).  In order to graduate in 4 years I had to take classes at the CC during the summer and take January and May minisession classes AND take 21 credits the fall semester of my senior year.  BUT I DID IT!!!!

6.  And 2 weeks later I married my high school sweetheart Scott.  I can honestly say that my married life with him has been truly charmed.  When asked how we liked married life shortly after we were married, we both responded  "it’s even better than I thought it’d be and I thought it would be good"  Ask me now and I’d say it just keeps getting better! 

7.  I taught third grade for 3 years at a small, private, Christian school. 

8.  In spring of 2004 I had my son and became a stay at home mom.

9.  In winter of 2006 I had my daughter.   It has been so much fun watching the two of them bond and connect as siblings.  They share a room and while they def have their moments of rivalry, I’d also say they are best friends. 

10.  In the summer of 2007 I had my second daughter.  She too is a delight and now that she is growing from baby to toddler I am really enjoying getting to know her and see her personality come out!

 

I guess if asked to sum up my last 15 years, I’d have to say the Lord has been very good to me.  I truly love my life as wife and mom of three and wouldn’t change a thing!  I am tagging Amy, Mrs Querido, Erin, Jessie, and Karen.  Your turns to reminisce and take us on a stroll down memory lane!
