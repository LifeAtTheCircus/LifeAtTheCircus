Under the Big Top (Part 2)

2008-05-12
Yesterday I posted about how similar our life is to the real circus we visited last week. I would now like to point out two unfortunate differences. At the circus they have these men dressed in all black that come into the ring between acts and clean up. They remove equipment, clean up poop, and set up for the next act. I need these men at my house. Imagine if after the meal (act 1) these men just instantly appeared and quietly, miraculously, cleaned up the mess when we move on to playtime (act 2).
